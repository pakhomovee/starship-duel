02 · Economy

Anatomy of a turn

Ships alternate turns.

What you get

  • 2 actions every turn.
  • Energy income at turn start.
  • Actions beyond the base 2 carry over.
  • The second mover starts with a small komi handicap.

What you spend it on

Actions come in three families:

  • Move & hold.
  • Score & strike.
  • Tools & unlocks.

Crash your bot or run out of time and you forfeit.
